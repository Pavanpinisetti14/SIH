Help Page: Registration and Login Instructions

1. Registration Instructions

Step 1: If you are new to our platform, visit the Sign Up Page to create a new account.

Step 2: Enter valid details such as name, email, and password.

Step 3: Agree to the terms and conditions, then click the "Register" button.

Step 4: A verification email will be sent to your inbox. Click the verification link to activate your account.

Step 5: After registering, you will be redirected to the Home Page. From there, you can log in using your credentials.

Go to Sign Up Page

2. Step-by-Step Login Instructions

Step 1: Once registered, visit the Login Page of the web scraping tool.

Step 2: Enter your username/email and password into the appropriate fields.

Step 3: If your credentials are correct, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

Go to Login Page

3. Troubleshooting Login Issues

  • Forgot Password? Click here to reset your password.
  • Forgot Username? Try logging in with your email address or retrieve your username here.
  • Still having trouble? Contact our support team at support@example.com.

4. Common Login Issues

  • CAPTCHA not loading? Try refreshing the page or using another browser.
  • Account locked? If you've entered incorrect credentials too many times, check your email for account unlock instructions.
  • Login page not loading? Clear your browser cache or try an incognito window.

5. Contact Support

Still need help? Reach out to our support team at support@example.com or visit our Support Page.
